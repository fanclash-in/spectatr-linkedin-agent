| `read_history(30)` | ✅ OK | Returned `[]` — Day 1, no history |

### Root Cause
The LinkedIn Marketing Developer Platform requires explicit OAuth 2.0 scope grants at the time of app authorisation. The currently connected token was either:
- Authorised by a user without Campaign Manager Admin access, OR
- Authorised before the required scopes were added to the app configuration, OR
- The token has expired and re-authorisation is needed

### Fix Instructions (< 15 minutes)
1. Navigate to **Settings → Integrations → LinkedIn Ads → Re-authorise**
2. Ensure the authorising LinkedIn user holds **Account Manager or Admin** role on the Campaign Manager account
3. During OAuth consent, confirm the following scopes are granted:
   - `r_ads` — Read campaign structure, budgets, targeting
   - `r_ads_reporting` — Read analytics: CTR, CPL, impressions, clicks, spend
   - `r_organization_social` — Read sponsored creatives, ad copy, headlines
4. After re-authorisation, manually trigger today's run again to capture a valid baseline

### Business Impact of Delay
Every hour the API remains unauthorised is an hour of live LinkedIn spend running without automated monitoring. CTR drops, CPL spikes, frequency fatigue, and budget over-delivery cannot be detected. If campaigns are currently active, this represents **unmonitored spend**.
